About

Bethany, native of Pennsylvania, is a self-made photographer of island scenary. Her career started with her travels to Tahiti, where she was inspired by the spiritual nature of the islands.

Her photos contain complex layers of greens, blues, with a smattering of reds and yellows that will keep you captivated. You will notice a recurring theme of water and earth, the primary focus of the majority of her work.

Bethany's goal is to instil in her viewer a sense of awe for the raw nature of the South Pacific. Take time to browse her portfolio and let the depth of the art sink into your soul.
